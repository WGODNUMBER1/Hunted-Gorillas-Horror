CREATURES THAT HUNT IN THE DARK
THE STALKER
Silent. Fast. Watches from corners. The Stalker only moves when you look away. If you hear breathing behind you, it’s already too late.
THE CRAWLER
Moves on walls, ceilings, vents. You never hear it coming — only the scraping of claws above your head. Avoid tight spaces.
THE ECHO
Mimics player voices. If you hear your own voice calling for help, do NOT respond. The Echo feeds on attention.
